Workouts

Boost your fitness with these simple workout routines:

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Squats: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Planks: 3 sets of 1-minute holds
  • Jumping Jacks: 3 sets of 30 reps

Nutrition

Fuel your body the right way with these nutrition tips:

  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vegetables, fruits, and lean protein.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking at least 8 glasses of water a day.
  • Avoid processed foods and sugary drinks.
  • Eat small, frequent meals to maintain energy levels.

Fitness Tips

Stay on track with these motivational fitness tips:

  • Set realistic goals and celebrate your progress.
  • Consistency is key—make fitness a habit.
  • Prioritize rest and recovery to avoid burnout.
  • Find a workout buddy to stay motivated.
